# 📊 Customer Spending Overview Dashboard (Power BI)

## 📌 Project Overview

This Power BI dashboard provides a comprehensive analysis of **consumer shopping behavior**, focusing on spending patterns, customer demographics, and purchasing preferences. It helps identify key trends that support data-driven business decisions.

---

## 🎯 Key Objectives

* Analyze customer income and spending behavior
* Understand shopping preferences (Online, Store, Hybrid)
* Evaluate the impact of customer psychology (impulse buying, discount sensitivity)
* Compare performance across different city tiers and demographics

---

## 📈 Dashboard Highlights

### 🔹 KPI Cards

* **Average Income:** 132K
* **Average Orders:** 25
* **Total Customers:** 12K

These KPIs provide a quick summary of overall customer behavior.

---

### 🔹 Shopping Behavior Analysis

* **Shopping Preference Distribution:**
  Majority of customers prefer **Store shopping (~87%)**, followed by Hybrid and Online.

* **Impulse Buying vs Product Availability:**
  Shows how impulse buying scores influence product availability across shopping modes.

---

### 🔹 Demographic Insights

* **Orders by Gender:**
  Orders are evenly distributed across Male, Female, and Other categories (~33% each).

* **Revenue by City Tier:**
  Tier 1, Tier 2, and Tier 3 cities contribute almost equally to total revenue.

---

### 🔹 Financial Insights

* **Income Group vs Spending:**
  High-income customers contribute significantly more to both online and store spending.

* **Revenue by Delivery Type:**
  Slow delivery generates higher revenue compared to fast delivery.

---

### 🔹 Customer Behavior Trends

* **Discount Sensitivity vs Online Orders:**
  Online orders remain relatively stable across different discount sensitivity levels.

---

## 🎛️ Filters (Slicers)

The dashboard includes interactive filters for:

* Gender
* City Tier
* Age Group
* Shopping Preference

These allow users to dynamically explore data across different segments.

---

## 🧠 Key Insights

* Store shopping dominates customer preference
* High-income customers drive the majority of revenue
* Delivery speed impacts revenue generation
* Customer behavior is consistent across demographics

---

## 🛠️ Tools Used

* Power BI (Data Visualization)

* Data Cleaning & Transformation (Power Query)

## 🚀 Conclusion

This dashboard provides actionable insights into customer behavior, helping businesses optimize marketing strategies, improve customer targeting, and enhance overall sales performance.
